Data controls

Your data is always stored locally in this browser. When cloud sync is configured and you are signed in, a private user-specific copy is also stored in Supabase and synchronized across devices. The tax engine is an estimate for planning and is not payroll, legal, or tax advice. Employer withholding can differ due to W-4 details, payroll treatment, benefit taxability, nonresident-alien rules, and partial-year residency.
